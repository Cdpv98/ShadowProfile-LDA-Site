Royal Wars: Farm TD

Royal Wars: Farm TD offers a fresh twist on tower defense with a unique blend of strategy, luck, and resource management. Jump into the action and defend your kingdom against relentless waves of enemies using an innovative system that challenges you to think on your feet!

Description

Key Features:

Random Tower Selection:

  • Place your towers strategically, but expect the unexpected! Each tower is randomly selected from a pool, forcing you to adapt your tactics with every placement.

Tiered Tower System:

  • Merge identical towers to unlock higher-tier versions! Combine two Tier 1 towers to get a random Tier 2 tower. Continue merging up to Tier 5, each bringing new powers and abilities to your defense.

Wave-Based Combat:

  • Survive increasingly challenging waves of enemies. With each wave you overcome, earn gold to bolster your defenses and prepare for the next onslaught.

Resource Management:

  • Earn gold by defeating enemies and surviving waves. Use your gold wisely to plant crystals or place towers.

Crystal Harvesting:

  • Plant red, blue, and green crystals that grow over two rounds. Harvest them at the end of their cycle, yielding a random number of crystals. The market value of crystals fluctuates each round, so sell at the right time to maximize your earnings!

Dynamic Economy:

  • The value of your harvested crystals varies from round to round. Play the market carefully to maximize your kingdom’s wealth.
