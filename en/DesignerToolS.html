Designer ToolS - Easy Object Placement Tool

Design stunning levels in Unity with Designer ToolS! Features like Physics Placement, Randomization, and Fast Parenting give you full creative control for building immersive worlds effortlessly.

Description

Physics Placement: Take advantage of Unity's physics system to position objects more naturally, taking gravity and collisions into account for more realistic behavior.

Collision Positioning : Precisely place objects on mesh vertices, edges, or faces that have physical colliders.

Precision Mode: This mode allows for exact positioning of unique objects where details are critical.

Multiple Placement Mode: Quickly scatter multiple objects at once and choose what object will be painted more frequently than another, perfect for populating expansive outdoor environments.

Eraser Mode: Use the eraser Mode to remove objects instantly.

Surface Alignment: Automatically rotate and align prefabs to match the surface angle of the placement area for a seamless and natural look.

Dynamic Randomization: Bring variety to your scenes with random scale, offsets and position offsets for your objects.

Automatic Organization: Use the parenting to neatly group placed prefabs under relevant parent objects.

Angle-Based Filtering: Control placement on sloped surfaces with slope or angle restrictions.

Grid Snap: Create uniform object arrangements with an adjustable grid system.

Vertex Snap: Snap objects directly to mesh vertices for seamless alignments.

Easy Prefab Selection: Instantly choose prefabs from the scene for fast, intuitive placement decisions.

Shortcuts: Improve your efficiency with quick-access keyboard shortcuts.
