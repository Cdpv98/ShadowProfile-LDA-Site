Shadowed Descent

Shadowed Descent blends fast-paced action with strategic elements in a dark and immersive journey. Face relentless enemy hordes and solve puzzles as you uncover the mysteries of a haunted world. Rely on your wits, quick reflexes, and resource management to overcome each challenge in this unique game that puts your skills to the test!

Description

Immersive Experience:

  • Realistic graphics and sounds for a better experience, a minimalist interface that guarantees a totally immersive experience that will always keep you attentive to new events.

Equipments:

  • Use equipment such as a flashlight, lamp and their respective charges, you also have access to a VHS player and a TV to watch tapes left by the last residents.

Locations:

  • Explore all the haunted buildings spread across the town looking for clues and hauntings.
