Lighting Occluder

Lighting Occluder boosts game performance by culling distant lights and shadows, optimizing lighting calculations for smoother gameplay while preserving visual quality in your game.

Description

Lighting Occluder is a tool designed to optimize lighting in your game, significantly enhancing performance while maintaining high visual quality. This asset offers a comprehensive lighting management solution. Key Features:

Dynamic Light and Shadow Culling:

  • Utilizes an animation curve to intelligently cull distant lights and shadows, reducing unnecessary calculations.
  • Improves performance by minimizing the rendering load, ensuring a smoother gameplay experience.

Global and Local Light Management:

  • Global Manager: Set up a global manager to control all lights within the player or camera, streamlining your lighting setup.
  • Local Settings: Customize light definitions for specific lights, providing flexibility and precision in your lighting configuration.

Performance Boost:

  • Our extensive testing shows a performance increase of 30~40 FPS, with the potential for even greater gains depending on your scene complexity and lighting setup.
  • This optimization allows for more resources to be allocated to gameplay mechanics and graphics.

Easy Integration:

  • Seamlessly integrate with existing projects without major adjustments.
  • User-friendly interface for quick setup and customization.
