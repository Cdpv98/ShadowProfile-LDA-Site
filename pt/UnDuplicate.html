UnDuplicate

The Duplicate Asset helps identify and manage duplicate files in your project. It checks references across open and closed scenes, and provides options to safely delete selected duplicates.

Description

The UnDuplicate asset is an essential tool for keeping your Unity project clean and efficient by identifying and managing duplicate files. With a simple drag-and-drop interface, you can check for references across both open and closed scenes, ensuring that no essential assets are deleted by mistake. The tool offers clear feedback, categorizing duplicates as either "Referenced" (still in use within scenes or assets) or "Safe To Delete" (no references found). Once duplicates are detected, you can easily select and delete the unnecessary files, helping to free up space and improve project organization. The process is streamlined with a progress bar, and you can cancel the operation at any time if needed, ensuring full control over your asset management.
