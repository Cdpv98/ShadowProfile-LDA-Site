Who we are

We are a game development company combining creativity and technology to craft innovative experiences. We offer services such as Unity 3D and Unreal Engine development, NFT games, and augmented and virtual reality experiences for platforms like Apple Vision Pro and Meta Quest. With a passionate and dedicated team, we focus on creating games that blend compelling storytelling, dynamic gameplay, and stunning visuals. We thrive on the passion for transforming ideas into unique digital adventures.

Royal Wars - Farm TD

Royal Wars: Farm TD is a free-to-play tower defense game where strategy meets luck. Place randomly selected towers, merge them to unlock higher tiers, and defend your kingdom through multiple levels. Plant crystals, manage resources, and prepare for epic battles.

Pepe Pinguino

Pepe Penguino is an immersive WebGL endless runner game, expertly designed to adapt seamlessly across various screens and gaming platforms. Engage in the thrilling experience as you help Pepe Penguino devour delectable fish. Navigate skillfully to dodge obstacles and gather an array of astonishing power-ups to enhance your gameplay!

Lussa

Lussa: The Final Frontier is a WEB3-oriented NFT PC / Mobile / Console game for real game lovers, enhanced with new dynamics and realistic graphics.

Novo Pangea

Novopangea is a multi-player economy style strategy game. The game is designed around real world mechanics such as renting land, owning a business and completing jobs.

Dedos

Tabletop game made with Unity. Compatible with web and mobile devices.

Chomp Chomp™

CHOMP CHOMP™ is a falling blocks puzzle game, where the player must connect blocks of animals and food. When an animal is matched with its favorite food, he starts eating the food, this also makes random blocks fall into the opponent game board.
